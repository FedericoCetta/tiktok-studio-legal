Privacy Policy — TikTok Studio

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Overview

TikTok Studio ("the App") is a personal, single-purpose tool used exclusively by its developer to publish short promotional videos to the developer's own TikTok account. The App does not serve third-party users and does not collect, store, sell, or share data from any person other than the developer.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal data from end users.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, cookies for advertising, or third-party trackers.
  • We do not sell or share any data with advertisers or brokers.

3. Information Processed Locally

When the developer uses the App, the following data is processed only on the developer's own machine and is not transmitted to any third party other than the explicit service providers listed below:

  • Images uploaded by the developer (stored temporarily in a local folder, deleted after use).
  • Text scripts typed by the developer, sent to the TTS provider to synthesize audio.
  • Generated MP4 video files (stored temporarily in a local folder).
  • OAuth2 access tokens received from TikTok, stored in an in-memory session and discarded on restart.

4. Third-Party Services

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to upload videos to the developer's own TikTok account. See TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs — for text-to-speech generation. Only the text scripts typed by the developer are sent. See ElevenLabs Privacy.

5. Data Retention

Local files (images, audio, video) are stored in a temporary folder on the developer's machine and deleted when the session ends. Access tokens are never persisted to disk.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for and is not used by children under 13.

7. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
